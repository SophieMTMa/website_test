GiftGenie

Gift Genie is a recommendation system that suggests gifts based on user input. Allows users to personalize gifts based on the recipient's age, gender, personality, and price range in US dollars. Used the Cohere LLM and hosted on Streamlit.

ResQlearn

Tackle the issue of the lack of emergency knowledge taught in our traditional education system. Designed a gamification of traditional emergency skills' education, ResQLearn, for children to learn vital rescue techniques in fun and innovative ways.
